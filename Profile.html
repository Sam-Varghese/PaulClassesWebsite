About Paul Classes

Paul Classes was made in order to give students the personal attention that they require so that they may have their foundations very strong which would automatically devlop their interest in that subject. Paul Classes is availible for both online and offline mode. Students who wish to study from the comfort of their home, may opt for online mode, while students who are not comfortable with online classes may opt for offline classes.

You are welcomed to attend free demo live classes till your ward feels comfortable joining this class. Both online and offline modes are good, but to avoid transition from one place to other, online is recommended.

Your ward is expected to just have an android phone with 4G speed DATA of 0.5 GB per day. Regular weekly and monthly tests would be conducted in both OFFLINE and ONLINE mode. It would be compulsory for every student to keep their cameras ON and respond whenever a question is asked.
